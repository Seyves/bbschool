Why enroll in Digital Business program
Our mini-mba program in Digital Business is tailored to guide business leaders to effectively bring about digital transformation in their organizations. As part of the program, participants explore and learn from real-world case studies such as Google, Netflix, Intuit, SAP, and many more. Participants will create, discuss and present the digital strategy for the chosen company during the course Boost your Digital Quotient and lead your organization from being part of the herd that merely engages in digital to a remarkable, innovative enterprise.

Modules

Module 1
Digital Transformation

The first module covers challenges of the digital age, digital business models, domains of digital transformation and five customers behaviors in the digital world.

You will also understand how to work with the Big Data and learn the principles of successful innovation.

10 weeks
Module 2
Digital Business Models: Executing a Digital Transformation

In this module, you will learn how to manage disruptive business models, six incumbent responses to a disruptive challenger, and the customer value and network effects.

We will discuss how to launch and grow digital platforms, govern them and design the right digital strategy for the traditional industry.

Your will learn how to create the roadmap for the digital transformation based on Digital Transformation Maturity Model 4.0

10 weeks
Module 3
Digital Marketing Strategy

We will speak about planning and executing digital marketing strategies and align them with the company's communication strategy.

This module also includes disciplines that cover information about channels and principles of digital advertising, how to measure the impact of marketing and top brands' practices with social networks.

10 weeks
