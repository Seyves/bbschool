About the Business School

Boston business school was established in 1914 to provide technical training education to the people of Boston, Massachusetts.


In 2020 the School has reopened its doors as an International educational facility, keeping the focus on teaching advanced business programs online such as MBA and Mini-MBA. The first international program has been launched at the beginning of 2021.


For the past 20 years our tutors have accomplished more than 200 projects that have been transposed into practical cases.

Our programs are based on the fundamental educational MBA standards and being continuously adapted according to the latest trends of top business schools

We have a good mix of online educational tools and practices, from webinars and self-assessment , to peer2peer assessment and

We use practical templates to solve real business cases all prepared by the leading consultants and experts, using both local and international companies

We invite speakers from leading companies and industries to share their knowledge and experiences on the online webinars
