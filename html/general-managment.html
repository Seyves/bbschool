Why enroll in General Management
The General Management program provides current managers and executives tools and instruments, developes skills for understanding the core concepts of management theory and its successful implementation for result-oriented management. Participants will create, discuss and present the business strategy for the chosen company during the course. This course features lectures about defining your own style in management, effective internal and external communication, motivation, social competences, team development, best practices and recent trends. All the disciplines incorporate the analysis of real cases and experiences of top-executives. Students will explore the disciplines using discussions with peers and professors, collaborative work, personal and team projects.

Modules

Module 1
General Management

We will speak about key concepts in general management that enable you to understand your position and managerial responsibility as well as to find an appropriate management style that will correspond with your personal positioning.

The module will cover the latest trends and information in management, key managerial decisions and result-oriented management. We will also cover best practices of top-managers.

5 weeks
Module 2
Leadership & Motivation

The second module will cover understanding of your own leadership qualities in a result-oriented way, the elements of social competency and team-development.

You will learn how to motivate employees long-term and communicate both internally and externally.

5 weeks
Module 3
Strategic Management

In this module we will speak about the concepts of strategy, profitable growth, making minimal, but correct changes and creating structures.
The other part of module will cover strategies for cost leadership, specialization, differentiation and niche strategies.

We will discover the relationship of strategy, structure and processes that will help you successfully implement your strategies.

6 weeks
Module 4
Marketing Management

This module will cover the following topics:
- customer value in light of new technologies and social media,
- how does branding have to be carried out and
- what does «integrated communications» mean.

4 weeks
Module 5
New Business Development & Risk Management

This module will cover the following topics:
We will discuss new business models, formulate future-oriented strategies together, growth and profit drivers, business development, increased risk during times of high fluctuation, risk evaluation and companies financial flexibility.

We will also learn quick reactions to change and how to implement controlling tools.

6 weeks
Module 6
Managing Innovation, Information Technology, Digital Business and the Internet

The last module contains understanding how digitalization influences firms' business models, their competitive advantage, the industry structure, foundational institutions upon which industries operate, discovering key aspects of multi-sided businesses and platforms.

You will learn how to analyze or design a multi-sided business and lead your company to foundational transformations.

6 weeks

Requirements for applicants

  1. Proven business executives who hold leadership positions one or two levels from the CEO in large organizations and have been identified as central to the company's succession plan—including members of executive committees, heads of major business units, senior heads of functions, and senior members of the operating group

  2. Executives who hold C-suite level positions at midsize companies with significant growth opportunities.

  3. Particularly appropriate for senior executives with at least 10 years of work experience, including significant time as a senior executive or company officer.
